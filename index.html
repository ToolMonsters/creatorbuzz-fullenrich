Key Feature Requests

  • Controls for AI prompts/styles and explainable outputs linked to source signals.
  • Campaign-level dashboards with KPIs (CTR, conversions, pipeline impact) and exportable reports.
  • Configurable enrichment playbooks with triggers (import, update, campaign start) and scheduled runs.
  • Role-based access, approval workflows, and comment threads on records.
  • Expanded data sources (firmographic/technographic/intent) with regional coverage details.
  • Native HubSpot/Salesforce connectors with two-way sync, plus webhooks and a well-documented API.
  • Field-level validation, confidence scores, and automatic de-duplication/matching during enrichment.
  • SOC-style audit logs, consent handling, SSO/SAML, and GDPR-friendly data processing.

Main Criticisms

  • Insufficient control/explainability of AI outputs for business use.
  • Confidence on enriched fields is unclear; users want stronger validation and deduping.
  • Permissions and review flows are not granular enough.
  • Coverage gaps for certain regions/industries limit use-cases.
  • Workflows are hard to automate end-to-end without native triggers/scheduling.
  • Impact reporting is shallow; marketing needs KPIs and pipeline attribution.
  • Connectors feel limited; teams need reliable two-way sync and webhooks for real-time updates.
  • Buyers expect clearer compliance posture and admin controls.

User Recommendations

  • Offer guided prompts, style presets, and source citations.
  • Expose confidence scores and add automatic merge rules for duplicates.
  • Add role templates and approver workflows for teams.
  • Publish a live coverage map and recently added sources.
  • Prioritize robust HubSpot/Salesforce two-way sync and publish webhook examples.
  • Create a 15-minute quickstart and in-app checklist to first value.
  • Ship starter playbooks (import → enrich → route) with trigger options.
  • Clarify tiers by seats/volume and include a low-friction trial.

User Expectations

  • Fine-grained control over AI behavior and traceable outputs.
  • Reliable, accurate enrichment with visible confidence levels.
  • Smooth collaboration with role-based permissions.
  • Broad, up-to-date data coverage where they operate.
  • Guided onboarding and easy, self-serve help.
  • Clear visibility into results and business impact.
  • Automation that removes manual steps from repetitive tasks.
  • Transparent pricing that matches team needs.
  • Plug-and-play CRM integrations and an open API.
  • Fast processing and dependable uptime.
